Support Center

FAQ

How does TicketHub work?

TicketHub helps customers discover live events, choose seats on interactive venue maps, reserve tickets and complete payment online. After payment, the customer receives access to a digital ticket with event details and QR code.

Can I choose my exact seat?

Yes. For events with seating, TicketHub provides an interactive seat map where you can choose available seats, zones and ticket classes such as VIP, Gold and Standard.

When is my ticket confirmed?

Your ticket is confirmed after successful payment. Once payment is completed, the ticket status is updated and the digital ticket becomes available in My Tickets and in your account.

What payment methods are available?

TicketHub supports online payment through PayPal Checkout. Crypto payment is also available for selected orders, depending on the payment option shown on the checkout page.

Where can I find my purchased tickets?

All reserved and paid tickets are stored in My Tickets. Registered customers can also view their ticket history, spending, saved discounts and digital tickets in the personal account dashboard.

What is the difference between VIP, Gold and Standard tickets?

VIP tickets usually offer the closest view, premium areas or special access. Gold tickets provide a balanced view and comfort. Standard tickets are the most affordable way to attend the event. Exact benefits may vary depending on the venue and event type.

Can I book seats next to someone?

Yes. TicketHub keeps selected pairs of nearby seats available where possible, so customers can reserve two seats next to each other for concerts, stadium events, races and sports arenas.

Is TicketHub growing?

Yes. TicketHub is actively expanding with new events, improved seat maps, digital ticketing, secure online payments and a better customer account experience. More features and event categories will be added over time.
